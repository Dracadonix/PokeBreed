TERMINOLOGY

IVs: Genetic potential (0-31). Use the Terminal to plan 0-Atk or 0-Spe sets.

Natures: Stat multipliers (10%). Planned via Everstone logistics.

Egg Groups: Breeding compatibility logic. If two slots share a group, the Nexus Tree will flare.

Synergy Matrix: Analyzes your 6 slots for elemental vulnerabilities and coverage gaps.

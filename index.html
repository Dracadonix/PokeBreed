BREEDING ENCYCLOPEDIA

01. THE GENESIS LAW

All Pokémon eggs hatch into the "Starting Stage" of their line. Even if you breed two Tyranitars, you will receive a Larvitar. Special cases (Babies) often require an Incense held by the parent.

02. ESSENTIAL ITEMS

  • Everstone: Guarantees the holder passes their Nature (e.g., Jolly, Modest) to the offspring.
  • Destiny Knot: Randomly selects 5 IVs from both parents to pass down, instead of the usual 3.
  • Power Items: (Bracer, Belt, etc.) Guarantees that specific stat is passed. Vital for forcing "0 Speed" or "0 Attack."

03. EGG GROUPS & MOVES

Pokémon can breed with any other species in their Egg Group. Female determines the species; Male determines the Egg Moves. This is how you get Dragon Dance on Tyranitar (Male Lapras + Female Tyranitar).

04. THE MASUDA METHOD

Breeding two Pokémon from different real-world languages (e.g., English Ditto + Japanese Charmander) significantly increases Shiny odds.

HIDDEN ABILITIES (HA):

  • Inheritance: Female Pokémon have a 60% chance to pass their HA. Males/Genderless only pass HA when bred with Ditto.
  • Ability Patches: Can unlock a Pokémon's HA permanently, but HA bred offspring will still follow the 60% rule.

EGG GROUPS: Pokémon can only breed if they share a Group. Ditto is the universal "Wildcard" exception.

0 ATK/SPE LOGIC: Used for Trick Room (0 Spe) or Foul Play/Confusion minimize (0 Atk).

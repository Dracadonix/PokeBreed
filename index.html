THE BREEDING BIBLE

1. The Pre-Flight Check

Grab a Flame Body Pokémon (Magmar/Talonflame) and put it in Slot 1. This halves your steps.

2. The Genetic Lock

Give the Everstone to the parent with the Nature you want (100% pass). Give the Destiny Knot to the other parent to ensure 5 IVs are passed down instead of 3.

3. The Incense Checklist

Hold these items or the baby won't hatch correctly: Sea (Azurill), Full (Munchlax), Lax (Wynaut), Rose (Budew), Wave (Mantyke), Rock (Bonsly), Odd (Mime Jr).

4. Hidden Ability (HA)

The female parent has a 60% chance to pass the HA. Males only pass HA when breeding with Ditto.

5. The 0-Stat Meta

Breed for 0 Speed for Trick Room teams and 0 Attack to take minimal damage from Confusion and Foul Play.

CHAMPION HALL: COMPETITIVE STRATAGEM

I. Speed Control

Speed is the most important stat. Use Tailwind, Trick Room, or Choice Scarf to manipulate turn order. 0 Speed IVs are mandatory for Trick Room.

II. Defensive Utility

Tera Types: Shift your type to surprise an opponent or shed a 4x weakness. Mega Evolution: Use the legacy stones for massive base-stat boosts.

III. EV Training

252 in two main stats, 4 in a third. Use Vitamins or Power Items. Never leave a Pokémon at Lv. 100 without 510 EVs.

I. The Everstone Mandate

The Everstone guarantees a 100% Nature pass-down. Without it, you are gambling with a 1/25 chance of failure.

II. The Destiny Knot Paradox

The Destiny Knot expands the IV inheritance pool from 3 to 5. This is non-negotiable for 5IV/6IV perfection.

III. Hidden Ability (HA) Logic

  • Female Parent: 60% chance to pass HA to offspring.
  • Male Parent: 0% chance UNLESS breeding with Ditto (60%).

IV. The 7 Incense Babies

Specific species WILL NOT hatch their baby forms without these held items:

  • Sea Incense: Azurill
  • Full Incense: Munchlax
  • Lax Incense: Wynaut
  • Rose Incense: Budew
  • Wave Incense: Mantyke
  • Rock Incense: Bonsly
  • Odd Incense: Mime Jr.

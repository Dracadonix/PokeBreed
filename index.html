The Breeding Bible: Omni-Redox

Mastery of Pokémon breeding is the ultimate expression of the competitive practitioner's skill. To understand breeding is to understand the very DNA of the VGC ecosystem.

I. Egg Group Taxonomy

Species compatibility is strictly dictated by Egg Groups. One must utilize a foreign Ditto to maximize efficiency and genetic diversity across these disparate biological classifications.

II. The Destiny Knot Paradox

The Destiny Knot ensures 5 IVs are inherited. This creates a statistical bottleneck where the breeder must constantly swap parents to "inch" toward the 6-IV or specialized 5-IV goal.

III. 0-IV Tactical Precision

A "No Good" Speed stat is more valuable than "Best" in Trick Room. Similarly, "No Good" Attack prevents unnecessary self-injury from Confusion. Precision is not always maxing—it's optimizing.

IV. Nature Locking

Everstones are non-negotiable. Without them, you are at the mercy of the 1/25 random nature roll. Secure your Adamant/Jolly/Timid core early in the process.

V. Mirror Herb Transfers

Immediate move acquisition via Mirror Herbs has made chain breeding a relic of the past. Simply enter a Picnic with an open slot and the herb equipped.

VI. Masuda Probability

Language-distinct breeding is the only viable path for shiny acquisition in high volumes. 1/683 odds are your baseline. Patience is your currency.

VII. Culinary Boosts

Do not breed without Egg Power Level 2. The time-to-egg ratio without boosts is unacceptable for the modern professional.

VIII. Discipline

A breeder must be detached from their results until the final hatch. Every mid-tier hatch is simply a stepping stone toward the 31-IV zenith.

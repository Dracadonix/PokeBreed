ENCYCLOPEDIA

01. The Lexicon (Common Abbreviations)

IV
Individual Values. Genetic potential (0-31).
EV
Effort Values. Stats gained through training.
HA
Hidden Ability. Rare traits passed by the Mother.
EM
Egg Move. Moves learned only via breeding.
OHKO
One-Hit Knock Out.

02. The Items of Power

Everstone: This is non-negotiable for Natures. If you have a Timid Ditto and want a Timid Charmander, the Ditto must hold this.

Destiny Knot: In nature, only 3 IVs pass down. This item forces the parents to pass 5 IVs. This is how "6-IV" Pokémon are created.

Power Items: (Bracer, Belt, Lens, Band, Anklet, Weight). Used in breeding to guarantee a specific IV passes. 100% success rate.

03. The Mother's Law

The Species is always determined by the Mother. If you breed a Female Tyranitar with a Male Lapras, the result is always a Larvitar (Genesis Law).

04. Egg Groups

Pokémon cannot breed with just anyone. They must share an Egg Group (e.g., "Monster," "Dragon," "Field"). Ditto is the "Universal Donor" and can breed with any group except "Undiscovered."

05. The Masuda Method

Named after Junichi Masuda. If parents are from different real-world regions (e.g., a French Ditto and an English Pikachu), the Shiny rate increases by roughly 500%.

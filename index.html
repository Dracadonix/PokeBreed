II. Statistical Inheritance (IVs & Natures)

Professional breeding involves moving from chance to absolute control. Individual Values (IVs) range from 0 to 31.

  • Destiny Knot: Guarantees 5 out of 12 combined parent IVs are passed down.
  • Everstone: Provides a 100% guarantee that the parent's Nature is inherited.
  • Tactical Minimalism: Professionals often seek 0 Speed for Trick Room or 0 Attack to minimize Foul Play damage.

III. The Mirror Herb Revolution

In Paldea, the Mirror Herb allows for immediate Egg Move transfer between any two Pokémon in a party during a Picnic, regardless of species.

  1. Target must have an empty move slot.
  2. Target must hold the Mirror Herb.
  3. Picnic with a Pokémon that knows the desired move.

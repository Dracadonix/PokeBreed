THE BREEDING BIBLE

IVs (Individual Values): The genetic DNA of a Pokémon. Ranges from 0 (No Good) to 31 (Best). Power Items can force these to pass.

Nature: Affects stats by 10%. Use an Everstone to pass this down from a parent 100% of the time.

Destiny Knot: When held by either parent, the egg will inherit 5 random IVs from the combined pool of both parents.

Genesis Law: No matter how powerful the parent, the egg will always hatch into the base evolution (e.g., Gengar produces Gastly).

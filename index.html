I. MOVE ACQUISITION METHODS

🐣 EGG MOVES: Inherited through breeding. Mirror Herb (Gen 9) allows instant transfer. Chain breeding uses intermediate species in same Egg Group.

📀 TM MOVES: Technical Machines. Crafted or found in overworld. Universal across most species.

📚 TUTOR MOVES: Taught by NPCs in exchange for resources (usually LP/Blueberry Points). Appeared in every generation since Gen III.

📈 LEVEL-UP MOVES: Learned naturally as Pokémon gains experience. Evolution often unlocks new moves.

II. HABITAT LOCATIONS

Each Pokémon has specific habitat zones. Knowing where to catch them is essential for obtaining 0-IV Dittos, specific natures, or hidden abilities.

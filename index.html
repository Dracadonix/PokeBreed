Statistical Inheritance: IVs & Natures

Individual Values range from 0 to 31. Since Gen VI, the Destiny Knot guarantees that 5 of the parents' combined IVs will be passed to the offspring.

The Everstone provides a 100% guarantee that the parent holding it will pass its Nature down.

Egg Moves & The Mirror Herb

In Paldea, the Mirror Herb allows for the immediate transfer of an Egg Move between any two Pokémon during a Picnic. The target must have an empty move slot and hold the herb.

In Gen II, DVs (now IVs) dictated shininess. In Gen V, Hidden Abilities were introduced via the Dream World, though initially only females could pass the trait.

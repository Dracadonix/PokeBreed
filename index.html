THE GENETIC BIBLE

I. Lexicon & Slang

IVs Individual Values (The 0-31 "DNA" of a Pokémon).
EVs Effort Values (Points gained from battling specific enemies).
HA Hidden Ability (Rare traits usually passed by the Female).
Egg Move Moves learned only by breeding with a different species.
6-IV A "Perfect" Pokémon with 31 in every single stat.
STAB Same Type Attack Bonus (1.5x damage for matching types).

II. The Gear (Source: Serebii/Bulba)

Everstone: If the parent is holding this, the baby WILL have the same Nature. Always put this on the parent with the nature you like.

Destiny Knot: Essential. It makes the baby take 5 stats from the parents instead of 3. Without this, getting a perfect Pokémon is nearly impossible.

Power Items: (Bracer, Anklet, etc.) These guarantee that a specific stat (like Speed) is passed down. Pro tip: Use this to pass a "0 Speed" for Trick Room teams.

III. The Genesis Law (Source: PokemonDB)

You cannot hatch an evolved Pokémon. Breeding a Tyranitar results in a Larvitar. Breeding a Charizard results in a Charmander.

Special Baby Clause: Some Pokémon (like Munchlax) require the parent to hold a specific Incense item, otherwise you will hatch the middle stage (Snorlax) instead.

IV. Competition Logic (Source: Smogon)

0 Attack: Preferred on Special Attackers to minimize damage from "Confusion" and "Foul Play."

0 Speed: Preferred for "Trick Room" teams so you move first in the slow-bracket.
